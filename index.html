Conversational AI Chatbot with Contextual Memory

  • Implemented LangChain's ChatMessageHistory to maintain conversation context across multi-turn sessions.
  • Document ingestion pipeline supporting PDF, TXT & CSV with dynamic Q&A using a custom prompt template.

Spam Classifier — NLP & ML Ensemble
